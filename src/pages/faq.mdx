## What is FailWarn?

FailWarn is a tool that helps you Track your website errors. It will notify you when an error occurs on your website.
We monitor your website from the client side, so you can catch errors that occur in your users' browsers.

## Is FailWarn free?

FailWarn is paid. We offer a 14-day free trial so you can try it out before you decide to pay. After the trial period, you will need to subscribe to a plan to continue using FailWarn.

## How does FailWarn work?

FailWarn works by adding a small script to your website. This script will track your website for errors and send you an email when an error occurs.
